2026

I really am working hard in 2026 for the things I want — a trait carried over from the previous year. I had my heart set on traveling abroad for school in the summer, and the universe made it happen.

This year, I'm moving away from my hometown, practicing letting go and reaffirming the value of connections at the same time, all while learning how to build my creative platforms. I'm painting more, taking care of my mental health, and always missing my youngest brother. I am endeavoring to read more than ever this year too, in a growing attempt to keep myself educated with and without formal institutions of learning.
